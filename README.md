# ZappQuizzy
ZappQuizzy is an online platform designed for both students and working professionals, providing a seamless experience for taking quizzes on various subjects such as programming, data structure, networking, and machine learning. This platform aims to enhance learning and test the knowledge of individuals in a convenient and interactive manner.

You can access the live version of ZappQuizzy at https://zappquizzy.pythonanywhere.com/.

## Features
* Diverse Quiz Categories: ZappQuizzy offers a wide range of quiz categories, including programming, data structure, networking, machine learning, and more. Users can choose quizzes based on their interests and expertise.

* User-Friendly Interface: The platform boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, ensuring a smooth navigation experience for users. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, ZappQuizzy caters to users of all levels.

* Real-time Feedback: Receive instant feedback on your quiz performance. ZappQuizzy provides real-time results and detailed explanations for correct and incorrect answers, aiding users in understanding the concepts better.

## Technologies Used
